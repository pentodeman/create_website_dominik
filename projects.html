VGA Frontend Board

2020 - Present

A device that converts standard VGA signals from a computer to raster scans that can drive XY displays.

Key Features

  • Standardized VGA input agnostic to sync polarity
  • High video gain that allows for generating strong blanking signals for CRT XY displays
  • Configurable design with inverse output signals for increased compatibility with different displays and oscilloscopes
  • Low EMI board design

Macro Keypad

Jan 2025 - Present

A macro keypad capable of emulating a keyboard with multi-keypress detection.

Key Features

  • Printing special characters or strings of text as a USB keyboard emulator
  • 4x4 keypad matrix of multiplexed Cherry MX Brown switches

  • Multiple keypress combination recognition

Automated Water Dispenser

Jan 2024 - Feb 2024

A desk-mounted water dispenser capable of detecting a water bottle and dispensing an exact amount of liquid from a preset.

Key Features

  • OLED display for feedback with real-time progress bar updates.
  • Modular PCB design
  • Empty and full bottle detection to determine if a pump should be started
  • USB-C PD power supply with voltage negotiation
  • Strain gauge for detecting bottle presence, current volume of water, and volume of water dispensed at any time
  • Volume and bottle presets

Truck Simulator Shifter Knob

Oct 2023 - Oct 2023

A truck simulator accessory that allows the user to turn any commercial simulator H-shifter and convert it to a semi truck layout.

Key Features

  • Compatibility with a wide range of H-shifters using threaded adapters (Moza, Thrustmaster, Fanatec, Logitech)
  • Gear split and range selector switches
  • 16-speed ZF transmission layout
  • Shifter knob from a MAN TGA semi truck
  • USB communication with gamepad emulation
  • Switch debouncing to limit a number of communication packets sent to the computer
